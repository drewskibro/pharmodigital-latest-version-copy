The Problem

Authority You Can't Compete With

National pharmacy chains like Boots and Superdrug have spent decades building authority. Thousands of locations. Millions in marketing. Established brands.

When patients searched 'HPV vaccine price London,' the big chains dominated every result. Not because their content was better — because their authority was overwhelming.

Sachin knew his clinic offered better service, more personalised care, and competitive pricing. But none of that mattered if patients couldn't find him.

Sachin was watching £50,000+ in annual revenue walk past his clinic — straight into Boots and Superdrug. He's now ranking for hundreds of keywords around HPV vaccines like Gardasil 9. He's found in AI overviews on the first page for far more than just one keyword.

The Discovery

AI Platforms Don't Care About Brand Authority

AI search is more meritocratic than traditional SEO: —but it's not completely level. Brand signals still matter. Domain authority still helps. But the advantage is smaller than ever..A two-person pharmacy with great content can beat Boots. Your clients prove it. The question is: how long until the big chains figure this out?".

We found thousands of monthly HPV vaccine price searches across London — patients asking specific questions about pricing, side effects, eligibility, appointment availability. The big chains had generic service pages. We saw an opening.

If we could create content that directly answered what patients were asking AI platforms, we could bypass decades of built-up authority.
